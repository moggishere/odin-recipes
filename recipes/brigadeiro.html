Brigadeiro

by: Panelinha

how long does it take to cook? very fast

how many units does it make? 30

a very popular sweet treat

Ingredients:

  • one tin can condensed milk
  • half measure of a can of milk
  • one spoon of butter
  • three spoonfuls of powdered milk chocolate mix
  • two teacups of granulated chocolate

Preparing:

  1. get thirty tiny cups for the brigadeiro.
  2. brush a plate with some butter. put it aside for now.
  3. separate the cups, organize them in rows on a platter.
  4. on a pot, mix the milk with the chocolate powder. but them under a low heat and mix until the chocolate is fully dissolved.
  5. mix the condensed milk and butter until it boils. from then, stir non-stop for 15 minutes or until the bottom of the pot becomes visible. remove the pot from the fire and transfer the brigadeiro to the buttered plate. let it cool off.
  6. on a plate, put the granulated chocolate and place it next to the brigadeiro.
  7. spread some butter to your hands and, with a teaspoon, make small spheres. roll them over the plate with chocolate, cover them entirely and put them inside the cups.
